I am launching this blog as a place to capture what I am learning and thinking about at the intersection of AI, cloud modernization, enterprise adoption, and practical transformation. I am especially excited by how quickly AI tools are becoming real creative and engineering partners: helping shape the positioning, write and revise content, generate HTML and CSS, prepare a Jekyll blog, process images, and publish the entire site through GitHub Pages. This site itself is a small example of that workflow: a personal Field CTO and AI transformation site built through an iterative human-plus-AI process, where the tools helped accelerate the mechanics and I kept refining the message, judgment, and professional tone.
